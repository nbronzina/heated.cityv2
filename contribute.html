Join the network

Every district in this atlas is documented by a local coordinator — someone who watches how care moves through their streets and writes it down. The network is looking for more.

1Pick a district

Four districts have coordinators waiting to publish: Lavapiés (Madrid), Carlton (Melbourne), Kibera (Nairobi) and Hauz Khas (New Delhi). Or propose a neighborhood you know well — the atlas grows wherever there is someone paying attention.

2Document six care projects

What exists in your district, who tends it, and how it changed everyday life. We send you the field template (the same structure every district uses), the image style kit, and the protocols already documented — adopt the ones that fit, and name the ones your district invented.

3Your district joins the atlas

Your neighborhood gets its page — map, projects, story — with you credited as its coordinator, alongside María, Himari and the rest of the network.
