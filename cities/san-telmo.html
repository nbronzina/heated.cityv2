San Telmo

Buenos Aires, Argentina

by María Fernández

San Telmo, one of Buenos Aires' oldest neighborhoods, has transformed its historic architecture into a laboratory for care-based climate adaptation. Cobblestone streets lined with 19th-century buildings now house thermal refuges, community water systems, and collective green spaces that preserve cultural heritage while addressing environmental stress through coordinated neighborhood care.
