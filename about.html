A speculative atlas of urban care infrastructure

Cities That Care is a speculative project by Heated Studio

Set between 2028 and 2030, it documents fictional urban districts where care functions as infrastructure. The focus is on how communities might adapt to climate change through local responses like thermal regulation, water management, and shared spaces.

The project includes observations from imagined neighborhoods that deal with environmental stress through systems of support, coordination, and adaptation. Examples include cooling centers, adaptive housing, and biointegration sites.

Descriptions are written from the perspective of local coordinators to give a sense of what everyday life might look like in these places.

All content is fictional and intended for research and imagination.

If you're interested in contributing or responding to the project, you're welcome to reach out.
